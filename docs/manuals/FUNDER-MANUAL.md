# Funder Dashboard Manual

## 1. Purpose
This manual is for `Funder` users.

The Funder Dashboard is designed to provide GDPR-safe, aggregated reporting only.

Funder users can access:
- `Funder Dashboard`

Funder users cannot access:
- `Admin Dashboard`
- `KPI Dashboard`
- `Custom Reports Dashboard`
- `ML Dashboard`
- general operational drill-downs with personal data

## 2. Signing In
1. Open the dashboard.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Change your password if prompted.

If you cannot access the dashboard, contact the dashboard administrator.

## 3. What You Will See
When you log in as a Funder, you will open directly into `Funder Dashboard`.

The page is designed to answer high-level funding questions such as:
- how many bids have been submitted
- how much funding has been raised
- what the overall funding trend looks like

## 4. Privacy and Data Protection
The Funder Dashboard is intentionally restricted.

It shows:
- aggregated counts
- aggregated funding totals
- grouped charts

It does not show:
- personal data
- participant-level records
- named attendee detail

## 5. Filters
As a Funder user:
- region is fixed to `Global`
- you can change the timeframe
- your assigned funder is automatically applied

Timeframe options:
- `All Time`
- `Year`
- `Quarter`
- `Month`
- `Custom Range`

## 6. Headline Metrics
The dashboard shows:
- `Bids Submitted`
- `Total Funds Raised`

These reflect the selected timeframe and your assigned funder.

## 7. Income Trend
The `Income Trend` chart shows aggregated funding movement over time.

It combines:
- payments
- grants

Use it to understand:
- whether income is stable or irregular
- whether recent periods show more or less activity

## 8. Last Data Refresh
The dashboard also shows the latest refresh timestamp.

Use this to confirm whether the figures are current enough for your reporting needs.

## 9. What Funders Cannot Do
As a Funder user, you cannot:
- switch to operational dashboards
- inspect personal records
- view event attendee detail
- run custom reports
- manage users

If you need a deeper export or narrative explanation, request it from an Admin or Manager.

## 10. Troubleshooting
### I see no data
- try a wider timeframe
- confirm your assigned funder has payment or grant records in the selected period

### The figures look out of date
- check `Last Data Refresh`
- contact the dashboard administrator if the refresh timestamp is too old

### I need participant-level evidence
- that is intentionally not available in the Funder role
- request a managed report from the organisation instead
